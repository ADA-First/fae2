Individual Subscription Levels

  • The Individual Subscription option of FAE is designed for inidividuals to create and manage their own accounts.
  • If an individual is consistently using FAE it is expected they would purchase a paid subscription based on their use and spidering needs to help support the delivery and enhancement of the FAE service.
  • To purchase a paid subscription you must be a registered user of FAE and then use Update Subscription page (e.g. in My Account) to create a subscription.
