{{start}} will help support the continued development of the tools and OpenAjax Evaluation Libary to provide evaluation results based on the latest web standards and most effective/efficient accessibility techniques to meet WCAG 2.0 Level A and AA requirements, including the emerging Accessible Rich Internet Application 1.1 and HTML 5.1 Specifications.

The funds will be used to:

  • Support graduate and undergraduate students in helping to develop and test the open source tools for evaluating and inspecting web accessibility (e.g. FAE, AInspector Sidebar and Accessibility Bookmarklets).
  • Development of online training materials to help people understand accessibility using web standards, including examples for the ARIA Authoring Practices Guide .
  • Support participation in web standards groups like the W3C Web Accessibility Initiative to learn about and contribute to the development and implmentation of the next generation web accessibility standards and techniques.
  • Support participation in regional and national conferences to help people learn about web accessibility and to promote the open source tools as a resource in people understanding and designing accessible web resourses.
